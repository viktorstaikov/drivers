Drivers Background Check system

You got two options for login: as a Driver or as an Admin.

As a Driver you can:

  • Register and fill out some info about you.
  • Log in and see your information. You will also see the status of your Background check.
  • Log out and see a "Bye" message.

The Admin account is a secret, but I'll tell you what you can do with it:

  • Adding a new driver.
  • Listing all drivers in the system with all the information about them, including the status of their background check.
  • Deleting or editing the info of a certain driver.
  • Exporting in CSV format the data for 'unchecked' drivers.
  • Importing in CSV format the data for background checks of drivers. If a driver no longer (or has never existed, but somehow is presented), nothing hapens.
